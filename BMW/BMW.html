BMW

BMW, established in 1916 and a key part of the BMW Group, is a prestigious German manufacturer known worldwide for its premium automobiles and motorcycles. Renowned for engineering excellence and dynamic performance, BMW has a long legacy of innovation and quality.

BMW Motorrad, the company’s motorcycle division, produces a diverse range of high-performance and luxury bikes, including iconic models like the S1000RR superbike, the versatile R1250GS adventure bike, and the sophisticated K1600B touring machine.

BMW motorcycles span categories such as naked, adventure, and sport bikes, all equipped with cutting-edge technology like ABS, traction control, and electronic stability systems, underscoring BMW’s commitment to rider safety and advanced engineering.
